Got Questions? Look Here

What is an OTC platform?

An OTC (over-the-counter) trading platform is a marketplace where buyers and sellers can trade financial assets without the use of a traditional exchange. It is a platform that allows traders to buy and sell directly with one another, rather than going through an intermediary.

How secure is Swapnotch?

All smart contracts involved in performing trades are secure, audited and verified by renowned auditing organizations. Furthermore, trades are only executed when the right conditions are met, thus creating a secure environment for our users.

What are the requirements for using Swapnotch?

You only require two wallets to perform a currency exchange: one with the currency you want to trade, and another with the currency you want to obtain.

Is Swapnotch decentralized?

Yes, our OTC exchange empowers users to take control of their trades and negotiate directly with each other, thus creating a more efficient and transparent market. Rest assured that there is no centralized organization involved in all your transactions.
